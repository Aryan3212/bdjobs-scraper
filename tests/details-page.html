SQA Engineer / Software Tester

Talent Pro

View all jobs of this company

Vacancy

Not specific

Job Responsibilities

  • Participate in product requirements analysis and design review;
  • Develop test plans, write and execute test cases, perform defect tracking and quality analysis;
  • Guarantee the quality of the system under test, and strive to improve product quality and R&D efficiency through testing process and method innovation;
  • Responsible for functional testing, performance testing, APP special testing, etc. of related projects;
  • Responsible for the automated development of test cases to improve test coverage and execution efficiency;
  • Assist development to locate bugs.
  • Doing best practice with automation testing
  • Ensure various testing like load testing, stress testing, regression testing with various tools
  • Develop test plans, write and execute test cases, perform defect tracking and quality analysis;
  • Guarantee the quality of the system under test, and strive to improve product quality and R&D efficiency through testing process and method innovation;
  • Responsible for functional testing, performance testing, APP special testing, etc. of related projects;
  • Responsible for the automated development of test cases to improve test coverage and execution efficiency;
  • Assist development to locate bugs.

Employment Status

Full-time

Educational Requirements

  • At least BS degree in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) from any reputed university.
  • Bachelor degree (inclusive) or above, more than 1 year experience in testing work, those with experience in software testing or development are preferred;

Experience Requirements

  • At least 5 year(s)

Additional Requirements

  • Age 25 to 40 years
  • Relevant professional Certifications would be a plus.
  • Good in communications with excellent English language ability.
  • Familiar with software testing methods and software engineering knowledge, familiar with defect management process and quality control;
  • Familiar with testing related technologies, familiar with tool frameworks such as Selenium, Postman, Jmeter, etc. is preferred;
  • Familiar with large database systems, able to use SQL language proficiently; Familiar with any language user such as PHP, Go, Java, Ruby, Python is preferred;
  • Strong learning ability, rigorous work attitude, high sense of responsibility, proactive, strong coordination and communication skills; good sense of teamwork, able to communicate effectively with project team members;
  • Proficient in automated testing, performance testing, strong technical ability;
  • Test Automation in Java or any other language
  • Experience in using JMeter and Junit
  • Various level testing experience with proper documentation
  • Experienced with related tools and technologies
  • Other Skills Requirements
  • Proficient in English (oral & written)
  • Good troubleshooting skills.
  • Continuous learner.
  • Proactive.
  • Possesses sense of ownership.
  • Interest in the work being done.

Job Location

Dhaka (Banani)

Compensation & Other Benefits

  • Weekly 2 holidays
  • Lunch Facilities: Full Subsidize
  • Festival Bonus: 2
  • Other benefits as per company policy.

Job Source

Bdjobs.com Online Job Posting.
